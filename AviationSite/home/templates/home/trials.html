Trial Sessions

We are running trial sessions for anyone who is interested in gliding and would like to try it out. The event includes transport to the airfield and a 20-30 minute flight with an instructor. A full safety brief will be given and you'll also be shown the various other ground handling activities that take place when gliding.

The cost of a session is only £35! Unfortunately, space is very limited.

Don't miss this great chance to take to the skies!
